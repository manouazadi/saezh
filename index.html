About

I humbly commenced on my cinematic journey as an amateur filmmaker, learning the art of filmmaking through experimentation and sheer passion for the craft. My professional path as a computer scientist and electronics engineer has refined my creativity and allowed me to blend technology and storytelling, which resulted in shaping my directorial approach.
